Redfine what is possible

7 iconic marathons 7 countries in 7 days

This isn't a race to the finish line. This is an epic adventure of achievement, camaraderie, breathtaking scenery.

Pushing yourself and your teammates to achieve something they never thought possible.

Day 1: Denmark

      Official Copenhagen Marathon
      Over 7,000 runners
      Experience the Inner city, multicultural Nørrebro and the ultra hip Vesterbro

Day 2: Sweden

      Söderåsen National park trail run
      Breathtaking scenery
      788 meters of climbing

Day 3: Germany

      1 of 2 a historical marathons
      Run along the old borders of Federal Republic and GDR
      UNESCO World Heritage Site

Day 4: Netherlands

      An energetic run in fancy dress!
      Explore Olympic Stadium, Anne Frank’s house, Sex and Cheese museum
      Ice cold beer straight from the Heineken brewery

Day 5: Belgium

      Brugge to De Haan
      The flattest of the 7 runs
      Crepes, chocolates and beer!

Day 6: France

      2nd historical marathon, run through Dunkirk
      15km beach stretch of bunkers and old ship remains
      A day to remember

Day 7: Name

      Spectacular views of the English channel
      Surrey Hills, Box Hill and Hampton Court
      Celebrate on the banks of the Thames River
